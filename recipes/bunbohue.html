Spicy Beef and Pork Noodle Soup Recipe

Bún Bò Huế

Let's get you cultured past the basic Pho and into the spicy beef and pork noodle soup! Bun bo Hue is a hidden Vietnamese gem that has yet to make it in mainstream American cuisine. It is a rich and spicy soup with deep layers of flavor, paired with tender slices of beef and pork.

Ingredients

  • 2lb beef shank
  • 2lb oxtail
  • 2lb pork hocks
  • 1lb pork sausage
  • 1lb block of pork blood
  • 12 stalks lemongrass
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 3 tablespoon salt
  • Mint
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Add the meat, broth, lemongrass and onions to the pot and fill with water almost to the brim. Bring to a boil then drop the heat to medium-high to maintain a low boil. Add the seasoning.
  2. Let it simmer and periodically check the meats for doneness and remove them as they finish cooking. The pork should be done after about an hour, the beef can vary between 2-3 hours.
  3. After all the meat has removed, let it cool, then slice it. Adjust seasoning and add water to the broth pot if necessary.
  4. Boil noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Assemble your bowl, and serve with herbs and veg on a side platter.
